Controls:

WSAD - camera movement on xy axis
EQ - camera movement on z axis
ZX - camera rotation
Numpad +- - object selection
Numpad 8456 - object movement xy axis
123 - object rotation

*direction
*ambient light

*color
*radius

*position
*normal
*color

*axis
*color
*radius

CONE
*position
*axis
*color
*angle

LIGHT
*position
